About

I am a third-year student at the University of Oregon pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration with a concentration in finance. I’m especially interested in financial modeling, Excel-based analysis, investment thinking, and financial advising.

My minors in economics and writing, public speaking, and critical reasoning strengthen the way I approach finance work. Economics helps me think more clearly about markets, incentives, and decision-making, while writing and public speaking help me explain financial ideas clearly to different audiences. Critical reasoning supports the analytical side of my work by improving how I evaluate assumptions, compare options, and make evidence-based judgments.

I want my portfolio to reflect both technical ability and communication skill, because strong finance work is not just about building a model or finding an answer. It is also about understanding context, explaining recommendations clearly, and helping people make better decisions.
